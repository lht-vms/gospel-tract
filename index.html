The Good News

A message of hope, love, and eternal life

Created for Glory

God created you with immense love and purpose. You were made in His image, designed to live in perfect relationship with Him—experiencing His love, joy, and peace forever.

"So God created mankind in his own image, in the image of God he created them." — Genesis 1:27
• • •

We Have Fallen Short

Because of our own free will, sin entered the world, and we all have fallen short of God's perfect standard. We've chosen our own way instead of His. We've acted in selfishness, pride, and rebellion. This separation from God is not just about mistakes—it's about a broken relationship that we cannot fix on our own.

"For all have sinned and fall short of the glory of God." — Romans 3:23

The consequence of sin is death—eternal separation from God. We cannot earn our way back through good deeds or religious efforts. Our constant sin has created a gap too great.

• • •

Jesus: The Living Vine

But God's love didn't stop there: He loved us more than the weight of our sin. In the greatest act of love in history, God sent His only Son, Jesus Christ, to bridge that gap. Jesus lived the perfect life we couldn't live, one without sin, and then He willingly died on the cross, taking the punishment for our sins upon Himself.

"For God so loved the world that he gave his one and only Son, that whoever believes in him shall not perish but have eternal life." — John 3:16

Jesus didn't just die—He rose again three days later, conquering death and proving His power over sin and the grave. Because He paid for our sins and defeated death, He offers us—all of us—eternal life.

• • •

His Amazing Love

Why would God do this? Because He loves you deeply and personally. Not because you earned it or deserve it, but because love is who He is. He knows everything about you—your joys, your pain, your regrets—and He still chose to die for you.

"But God demonstrates his own love for us in this: While we were still sinners, Christ died for us." — Romans 5:8

This is grace: undeserved, unearned love poured out freely. Jesus invites you to receive this gift, not through your effort, but through faith in Him.

"For it is by grace you have been saved, through faith—and this is not from yourselves, it is the gift of God—not by works, so that no one can boast." — Ephesians 2:8-9
• • •

Made Righteous

When you trust in Jesus, something miraculous happens. God sees you not as you are, but as Jesus is—perfectly righteous. Your sins are forgiven, washed clean. You become His beloved child—a child of God!

"God made him who had no sin to be sin for us, so that in him we might become the righteousness of God." — 2 Corinthians 5:21

And this isn't just about the future—it changes everything now. You are given new life, a new heart, and the Holy Spirit to guide and empower you. You can experience God's presence, purpose, and peace today.

• • •

Eternity with Him

That promise? It extends forever. When this life ends, those who trust in Jesus will spend eternity with Him in a place with no more pain, tears, or sorrow. A place of perfect joy, beauty, and love beyond imagination.

"He will wipe every tear from their eyes. There will be no more death or mourning or crying or pain, for the old order of things has passed away." — Revelation 21:4

This is the hope we have—a certain promise from the God who cannot lie.

Receive This Gift Today

Jesus is inviting you right now. He's knocking at the door of your heart. Will you open it?

Receiving Jesus is simple—it's not about reciting the perfect words, but about a sincere heart turning to Him in faith.

A Prayer to Begin:

"Jesus, I recognize that I am a sinner and have fallen short of Your glory. I believe You died on the cross for my sins and rose from the dead. I turn from my old life and ask You to come into my heart. Forgive me, change me, and make me new. I trust You as my Lord and Savior. Thank You for Your amazing love and the gift of eternal life. Amen."

If you prayed this prayer, welcome to the family of God! Begin by finding a community of Christians that follow Scripture and can help guide you, and start hearing from God today—by reading His Word, the Bible, and speaking with Him through prayer.

"Therefore, if anyone is in Christ, the new creation has come: The old has gone, the new is here!" — 2 Corinthians 5:17

Abide in the vine: Jesus. Through Him, the impossible was made possible. He can change your life, too!

"I am the vine; you are the branches. If you remain in me and I in you, you will bear much fruit; apart from me you can do nothing." — John 15:5

God bless!
